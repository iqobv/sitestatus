# About SiteStatus

## Our Mission

**SiteStatus** is a modern and transparent uptime monitoring tool built by developers, for developers. Our mission is to provide an objective, reliable, and easy-to-use solution that helps you stay informed about your service availability in real-time.

## Technology Architecture

We don't rely on a single point of failure. To ensure maximum accuracy and reliability, we have built a distributed infrastructure:

- **Regional Workers:** Powered by **Microsoft Azure**, our regional workers perform availability checks from multiple global locations to provide an unbiased view of your uptime.
- **Edge Performance:** We use **Turso** for edge data processing and **Neon (PostgreSQL)** for robust historical data storage, ensuring your monitoring stats are always fast and accessible.
- **Reliable Core:** Our backend is hosted on **DigitalOcean**, and our user interface is powered by **Next.js** on **Vercel** for optimal performance.

## Independence and Privacy

SiteStatus is an independent project developed and maintained by an **Individual Developer** from Ukraine. We prioritize your privacy by implementing **Google Consent Mode v2** and using secure, HttpOnly session cookies to protect your account.
